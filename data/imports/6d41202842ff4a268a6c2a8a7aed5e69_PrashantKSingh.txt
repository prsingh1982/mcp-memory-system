==>	Strides Pharma faced significant productivity challenges due to manual, legacy-driven processes and sought to modernize its 
operations through AI/ML-led transformation. I led the data and AI transformation initiatives across their drug 
manufacturing value chain, driving the adoption of intelligent automation and advanced analytics. Key implementations 
included AI-enabled automation within the Syncade MES, computer vision-based quality assurance for tablet inspection 
and automated, context-aware task creation from email communications to minimize operational delays. Additionally, I 
introduced contextual data analytics using ontological data models and enabled automated generation of QC scripts, 
significantly improving efficiency, accuracy and process standardization in a highly regulated manufacturing environment.

==>	Dr. Reddy's Laboratories aimed to accelerate its drug discovery and manufacturing innovation by leveraging AI for 
conversational interaction with experimental data, virtual lab simulations and rapid experimentation across varying control 
parameters. I designed a scalable, cloud-native AI platform on AWS to enable a plug-and-play approach for developing and 
deploying AI use cases across the pharmaceutical value chain. The platform supported multiple ML models and LLMs, 
incorporated a robust MLOps pipeline and included governance frameworks with built-in guardrails to ensure responsible 
AI usage and mitigate risks such as hallucinations. This enabled the organization to significantly accelerate innovation 
cycles, reducing the time to develop and deploy new AI use cases to approximately 30-45 days.

==>	Zydus Lifesciences sought to accelerate its R&D and manufacturing transformation by leveraging AI/ML for drug candidate 
identification, digitizing manufacturing processes, and modernizing its enterprise systems. I worked with their leadership 
team to architect a cloud-native transformation roadmap on AWS, enabling advanced analytics on large-scale molecular 
datasets using services such as Amazon SageMaker, Amazon Bedrock, and Amazon Q for Business to significantly reduce 
drug discovery timelines and costs. In parallel, I designed an AI-driven solution to transition from manual to Digital Batch 
Manufacturing Records (BMR), integrating with their CaliberIPQC MES to improve traceability, compliance, and operational 
efficiency, along with IoT-enabled predictive maintenance capabilities to minimize plant downtime. Additionally, I defined 
the end-to-end data and application migration architecture for their transition from SAP ECC to SAP S/4HANA, enabling 
global process standardization; the implementation was subsequently executed in collaboration with Accenture.

==>	Zahara Tours, a provider of curated travel experiences across Oman, sought to enhance its package creation capabilities by 
dynamically generating personalized travel itineraries based on customer preferences, historical behavior, cost optimization, 
and ancillary upsell opportunities. I worked with their leadership team to architect an advanced Agentic AI platform that 
leveraged a multi-agent orchestration model, with individual agents representing key ecosystem participants such as 
airlines, accommodation providers, transport services, and local guides. The system enabled end-to-end automation-from 
interpreting customer requests to intelligently composing optimized travel packages, and further assisting with booking 
completion across flights, stays, and activities. The solution was implemented using CrewAI for agent orchestration, 
powered by Llama 3.1 for language intelligence, and integrated with a Weaviate vector database leveraging the BGE large 
embedding model for contextual retrieval. This significantly improved personalization, increased conversion rates, and 
unlocked new revenue opportunities through intelligent upselling of travel experiences.
